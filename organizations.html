Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers (SHPE) SHPE changes lives by empowering the Hispanic community to realize its fullest potential and to impact the world through STEM awareness, access, support and development. Mexican American Student Association (MASA) The purpose of the Mexican American Student Association is to promote Mexican culture awareness, provide a network and support system for its members' academic and personal development, and promote multicultural awareness through the incorporation of all students, regardless of ethnicity, in presenting programs to aid tolerance and the interaction with other Hispanic and multicultural organizations. Social Chair - In charge of brainstorming ideas of MASA Socials
